Recipe finder app

-using api from "The Meal DB"

functionalities

1. search recipe by "name"
 - display matched meal image along with name 
 - on clicking a particular meal card, show details like, place of origin, the list of ingredients, step-by-step procedure and the youtube video(provided in the api) to follow etc..
2. click on "Surprise me!" button to generate random meal recipes
3. search meals by "main ingredient", "area" or "category"
4. save favourite meals in "my favourites" tab
5. dark mode toggle
6. weekly meal planner - let users add meals to a weekly planner in "My meal Plan" tab
